# Competitive Analysis of AI-Native Web Scrapers and Agent Browsers

## Executive summary

The market has split into four distinct layers. First are **AI-native ingestion tools** that optimize webpages into markdown or typed JSON for RAG and LLM pipelines. Second are **agentic browser frameworks** that let models act, observe, and extract from live pages with some degree of self-healing. Third are **production crawler stacks** that remain the most flexible option for teams willing to own orchestration. Fourth are **managed scraping APIs** that solve access, proxying, rendering, and anti-bot at scale but are still weaker on provenance-aware chunking and LLM-specific post-processing. That split is the central design insight: no current product is simultaneously best-in-class at access reliability, typed extraction, low-token output, deterministic replay, and cost-efficient high-throughput crawling.
